Significant Awards achieved by KMD Institute’s Students:

KMD Institute’s students and alumni have won several awards for their outstanding achievements such as:

  • Global High Archeiver , by NCC Education, UK

    This prestigious award is given to a student who has the highest mark for a programme throughout the world. KMD Institute’s students have won (32) Global High Achiever Awards.

  • Regional High Achiever , by NCC Education, UK

    This prestigious award is given to a student who has the highest mark for a programme within a region. KMD Institute’s students have won (34) Global High Achiever Awards

  • Roll of Honours , by NCC Education, UK

    This prestigious award is given to a student who has comparatively very high marks amongst the whole cohort. KMD Institute’s students have won (259) Roll of Honours Awards.

  • World First Award, by NCC Education, UK

    This prestigious award is given to a student who has the highest mark for a programme throughout the world. This award is the new version of the Global High Achiever Award, introduced in 2016. KMD Institute’s students have won (3) World First Awards.

  • World Second Award, by NCC Education, UK

    This prestigious award is given to a student who has the second highest mark for a programme throughout the world. This award was introduced in 2016. KMD Institute’s students have won (3) World Second Awards.

  • World Third Award , by NCC Education, UK

    This prestigious award is given to a student who has the third highest mark for a programme throughout the world. This award was introduced in 2016. KMD Institute’s students have won (1) World Third Award.

  • Level 4 Diploma in Computing (L4DC), by NCC Education, UK

    This prestigious diploma award is given to a student who has satisfactorily completed the L4DC programme awarded by the NCC.KMD Institute’s students have achieved (7827) L4DC Awards.
